Food Science March 2025

The Secret Behind Perfect Fermentation

Ever wondered why your grandmother's idlis were so soft and fluffy? The answer lies in the ancient art of fermentation. At KERA FRESH, we've mastered this time-honored technique to bring you batters that taste like home.

Natural fermentation is a magical process where beneficial bacteria and yeast work together to break down starches, create air pockets, and develop complex flavors. This is why fermented batters produce soft idlis, crispy dosas, and that distinctive tangy flavor that feels alive.

Why Natural Fermentation Matters:

  • Enhanced Nutrition: Increases bioavailability of proteins and vitamins
  • Better Digestion: Breaks down complex carbohydrates
  • Authentic Flavor: Develops the characteristic tangy taste
  • Perfect Texture: Creates air pockets for fluffy idlis

At KERA FRESH, we never rush this process. We give our batters the time they need - typically 8-12 hours - to ferment naturally at optimal temperatures. No chemicals, no shortcuts, just patience and tradition.

Tradition February 2025

From Stone to Spoon - The Legacy of Grinding

In South Indian homes, the rhythmic sound of stone grinding has been the morning soundtrack for generations. The traditional grinding stone (wet grinder) isn't just a tool - it's a guardian of flavor, texture, and nutrition.

While modern machinery has taken over most commercial food production, KERA FRESH stays true to tradition. We use stone-grinding techniques because they preserve what matters most.

Why Stone Grinding is Superior:

  • Temperature Control: No heat generation that destroys nutrients
  • Perfect Texture: Coarse enough to trap air, smooth enough to spread
  • Nutrient Retention: Preserves enzymes, vitamins, and minerals
  • Authentic Taste: The texture influences fermentation and final flavor

Every batch of KERA FRESH batter goes through this traditional process, monitored by experienced hands who understand the nuances of grain size, moisture content, and consistency. It takes longer, costs more, but delivers the authentic taste you remember.
