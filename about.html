Our Story

KERA FRESH is more than just a food brand - it's a bridge between South Indian culinary heritage and modern convenience. Rooted in centuries-old traditions, our batters and ready-to-cook foods bring the warmth, flavor, and comfort of home-cooked meals to kitchens across the UAE and beyond.

Founded in Ajman, UAE, in 2022 under South Lagoon Food Products LLC, KERA FRESH began as a humble initiative to serve the Indian diaspora longing for the authentic taste of home. Today, we proudly serve families, restaurants, hotels, and food businesses, delivering freshness, quality, and authenticity with every product.

The heart of South Indian cooking lies in patience - soaking, grinding, fermenting, and perfecting. KERA FRESH was born to honor those traditions while adapting them for modern kitchens. Crafted locally in the UAE, we combine traditional artistry with modern precision - retaining heritage while ensuring hygiene, safety, and convenience.

In a fast-paced world, we make it simple to enjoy wholesome, nutritious meals - preserving the soul of tradition while offering the ease of instant preparation.

KERA FRESH - because every meal deserves the warmth of authenticity.
