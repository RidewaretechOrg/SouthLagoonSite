Taste Tradition, Made Easy

Discover authentic South Indian recipes using KERA FRESH products. From classic breakfast favorites to quick snacks, create restaurant-quality dishes at home in minutes.

Tips for Best Results

Use a non-stick pan for dosas for even cooking

Steam idlis in a tightly covered vessel for fluffier results

Serve batters fresh within 3-4 hours of preparation for best taste

Breakfast 15 mins

1. Classic Idli

Soft, fluffy steamed rice cakes - a South Indian breakfast staple

Ingredients:
  • KERA FRESH Idli & Dosa Batter
  • Oil for greasing
Instructions:
  1. Grease idli molds with oil
  2. Pour KERA FRESH Idli Batter into molds (3/4 full)
  3. Steam for 10-12 minutes until cooked
  4. Let cool for 2 minutes, then remove

Serve with: Coconut chutney & sambar

Breakfast 10 mins

2. Golden Dosa

Crispy, golden crepe perfect for any meal

Ingredients:
  • KERA FRESH Idli & Dosa Batter
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Salt (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Heat a non-stick tawa on medium-high
  2. Pour a ladle of batter in center
  3. Spread in circular motion to make thin layer
  4. Drizzle oil/ghee around edges
  5. Cook until golden and crisp (2-3 mins)
  6. Fold and serve hot

Serve with: Potato masala, chutney & sambar

Snack 20 mins

3. Fluffy Medu Vada

Crispy outside, soft inside - the perfect savory donut

Ingredients:
  • KERA FRESH Vada Batter
  • Oil for deep frying
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a deep pan (medium-high)
  2. Wet your hands with water
  3. Take a portion of batter, shape into donut
  4. Make a hole in center with finger
  5. Gently slide into hot oil
  6. Fry until golden brown (3-4 mins)
  7. Drain on paper towel

Serve with: Coconut chutney & sambar

Breakfast/Dinner 15 mins

4. Appam with Coconut Milk

Lacy-edged, soft-centered Kerala delicacy

Ingredients:
  • KERA FRESH Appam Batter
  • Oil for greasing
Instructions:
  1. Heat appam pan, grease lightly with oil
  2. Pour a ladle of batter in center
  3. Swirl pan to spread batter on sides
  4. Cover and cook on medium heat (2-3 mins)
  5. Edges should be lacy, center soft
  6. Remove carefully

Serve with: Coconut milk, vegetable stew, or egg curry

Lunch/Dinner 5 mins

5. Malabar Parotta & Kurma

Flaky, layered flatbread - restaurant quality in 3 minutes

Ingredients:
  • KERA FRESH Half-Cooked Malabar Parotta
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
Instructions:
  1. Heat a tawa on medium heat
  2. Place half-cooked parotta on tawa
  3. Cook for 1.5 minutes on each side
  4. Brush with oil/ghee while cooking
  5. Press gently with spatula for flaky layers
  6. Remove when golden and crispy

Serve with: Spicy veg or meat kurma, chicken curry

Breakfast/Snack 10 mins

6. Instant Poori

Puffed, golden wheat bread - zero prep required

Ingredients:
  • KERA FRESH Ready-to-Fry Poori Discs
  • Oil for deep frying
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in a deep pan (medium-high)
  2. Gently slide one poori disc into hot oil
  3. Press gently with slotted spoon to help puff
  4. Fry until puffed and golden (1-2 mins)
  5. Flip and fry other side
  6. Drain on paper towel
  7. Serve immediately

Serve with: Potato masala, chole (chickpea curry), or any curry

Side Dish 5 mins

7. Coconut Chutney in Minutes

Fresh, authentic chutney - the perfect accompaniment

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup KERA FRESH Grated Coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1-inch ginger
  • Salt to taste
  • For tempering: mustard seeds, curry leaves, oil
Instructions:
  1. Blend coconut, chilies, ginger, salt with little water
  2. Grind to smooth paste
  3. Heat oil, add mustard seeds
  4. When they splutter, add curry leaves
  5. Pour tempering over chutney
  6. Mix well and serve

Serve with: Idli, dosa, vada, uttapam
