About Us – The KERA' Story

From Kerala Kitchens to UAE Homes – The KERA' Journey

KERA' was born from a simple question: How can we keep the soul of South Indian cooking alive in today's fast-paced world?

Rooted in Kerala's culinary heritage, we handcraft each batch of batter with care – using traditional stone-grinding techniques and natural fermentation methods. Made fresh in the UAE, KERA' delivers authenticity and flavor for families, professionals, and food lovers everywhere.
